With experience in management, logistics and professional services, he has positioned poultry production as a strategic lever to secure supply, create jobs and ensure products that meet institutional and household standards.

Our mission

At GL Zepoul Peyi, our mission is to provide a regular supply of fresh, safe and accessible eggs while applying rigorous sanitary and environmental standards.

We are committed to:

  • Deploy modern, supervised and controlled poultry production
  • Provide consistent quality that meets institutional standards
  • Support the local economy through value creation and jobs
  • Contribute concretely to food security in Haiti

Concrete commitments: controlled quality, batch traceability and planned delivery timelines.

Our approach

A clear method to ensure service continuity, sanitary quality and partner trust.

Sanitary quality

Hygiene protocols applied at every step to ensure safe, compliant eggs.

Reliable supply

Long-term partnerships

Trust-based relationships with institutions for regular, transparent service.

Our strategic vision

Our vision for GL Zepoul Peyi is to build a national reference company capable of sustainably supporting the country’s agri‑food independence.

  • Structured: A professional, stable and sustainable organization.
  • High-performing: Modern farming methods and controlled processes.
  • Responsible: A careful approach to the environment and animal welfare.
  • Recognized: A respected position in egg production in Haiti.
